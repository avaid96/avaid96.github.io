about

I am a current senior at Northwestern University. I love learning, creating things and seeing new places and meeting new people. I’m aiming to write about things that I learn, things that I create and just random thoughts and experiences.

Funny story, I first got into computer science because it was the only A/C classroom at my school. Escaping the Mumbai heat, I found myself enjoying the logical way computer scientists think and building tangible products that could solve problems. I’ve come a long way since then but not much has changed with respect to my curiousity, passion and excitement for learning new things, thinking, solving hard problems and showing off about something I’ve built. Besides my passion for technology, I am keenly interested in studying people’s choices through Economics and identifying opportunities using my financial skill set. I have a strongly entrepreneurial mindset and a passion for building things to solve problems. To know more about my education, experiences and projects: feel free to look at my CV

If you have any interesting work that you think I’d like, feel free to contact me. I am particularly interested in positions in product management
